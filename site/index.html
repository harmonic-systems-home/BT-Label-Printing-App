Design and print labels on your Mac.

A native macOS app for the Brother P-touch PT‑P300BT, over Bluetooth. Type, drop in icons or images, preview exactly what prints, and go.

Mac App Store (coming soon) Build it yourself

What it does

  • Live, exact preview of the printed label
  • Text with multiple fonts, Normal or Inverted styles, and smart sizing
  • Thousands of built-in icons with instant search
  • Images (PNG, JPEG, SVG, PDF) — or paste straight from the clipboard
  • Tape-color matching, so the preview looks like your loaded tape
  • Favorites with folders, plus an automatic history of what you printed
  • Multi-copy printing with automatic numbering and substitution tokens
  • iCloud sync across all your Macs

Free and source-available

BTLabel is source-available: anyone can read, modify, and build it for their own use from the GitHub repository for free. The Mac App Store version is a paid packaging convenience — a signed, notarized, auto-updating build with a one-time unlock for unlimited printing. Either way, the app is the same.
