## Description

BTLabel is a fast, native Mac app for designing and printing labels on the Brother P-touch PT-P300BT over Bluetooth — no clunky setup and no system printer to configure.

Design a label, see an exact preview, and print. Start simple with a single line of text, or turn on Advanced mode for multi-cell layouts, symbols, and images.

FEATURES
• Live, exact preview of your printed label
• Text with multiple fonts, Normal or Inverted styles, and smart sizing
• Thousands of built-in icons with instant search
• Add images (PNG, JPEG, SVG, PDF) — or paste straight from the clipboard
• Tape-color matching, so the preview looks like the tape you've loaded
• Favorites with folders, plus an automatic history of what you've printed
• Multi-copy printing with automatic numbering
• Substitution tokens for names, contact info, counts, and dates
• iCloud sync — your favorites, folders, and settings follow you across all your Macs

SUBSTITUTION TOKENS
Insert placeholders like /n (name), /p (phone), "/i of /c" (item numbering), or /d (date), and they fill in automatically — perfect for batches and dated labels.

GETTING STARTED
Connect your PT-P300BT from the Mac's Bluetooth menu, then click Status in BTLabel to confirm the connection. That's it — BTLabel talks to the printer directly.

A NOTE ON LANGUAGES
Labels use Latin-script text (English and Western European languages such as French, German, Spanish, Italian, and Portuguese). Other scripts can be added by pasting an image.

PRICING
BTLabel is free to try — design as much as you like and print up to 5 labels free. Unlock unlimited printing with a single one-time purchase. Family Sharing supported.
